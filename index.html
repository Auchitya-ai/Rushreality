What is Atharva Nagari-VIII?

Atharva Nagari-VIII is a meticulously planned 4-acre mega township located on Besa-Pipla Road in Nagpur. It's a premium township project developed by Atharva Infrastructures, offering a sophisticated living experience with thoughtfully designed luxury apartments that blend elegance with functionality.

Designed for comfort, convenience, and well-being, this community provides modern homes with top-class features and a wide range of amenities—delivering an elevated way of life for you and your family.

Atharva Infrastructures guarantees on-time possession with a legacy of successfully delivering 1500 flats prior to committed time. The project is RERA-registered (P50500053130) and approved by all financial banks, ensuring a secured home-buying experience.

Who is it for?

Ideal for families, professionals, and senior citizens who seek a secure, vibrant, and smartly designed residential community in Nagpur. If you value high-quality construction, eco-friendly features, and plentiful recreational options, Atharva Nagari-VIII is built with you in mind.
